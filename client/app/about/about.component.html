The GBG DEV TOURNAMENT is a competition for developers from all around the World.
During the intensive 3 days, teams compete in a variety of challenges and mind-twisting algorithm problems. The competition is based on matches between two teams, winner goes to the next challenge, looser goes home.
The Grand Winner will not only walk out cheered and covered in glory, but the team will also win a
$100.000 cash prize !
